The Cottage

Rooted in Nature,
Elevated in Comfort.

Welcome to Poplar Shores—a striking modern waterfront retreat designed for unforgettable stays. Set directly on the water, this architecturally refined cottage blends clean, contemporary design with the warmth of natural materials, creating a space that feels both elevated and inviting.

Wall-to-wall windows frame breathtaking lake views, filling the home with natural light and seamlessly connecting indoor and outdoor living. Start your mornings on the expansive second-storey deck as the sun rises over the water, spend your days steps from your private beach, and unwind in the evenings gathered around the fire beneath open skies.

With spacious, thoughtfully designed interiors perfect for families, couples, and groups alike, every detail has been curated to offer comfort without compromise. Whether you're here to relax, reconnect, or celebrate, Poplar Shores delivers a stay that is as effortless as it is unforgettable.
